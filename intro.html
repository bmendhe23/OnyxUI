Introduction

Get your desired web sites ready quickly and efficiently without much hasel. Onyx provides you with ready to use components that are also easy to re-style according to the user's need.

Installation

Onyx UI is very easy to integrate into your project by adding the given link of style sheet. Paste the below link in the head tag of your HTML document.

                        
                                                    <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://onyx-ui.netlify.app/css/style.css">
